# Product

## Register

product

## Users
International students preparing for the TestAS exam. They are often under high stress, preparing in a foreign language (German/English), and need to build speed and accuracy under timed pressure.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-fidelity mock testing and practice platform that simulates real TestAS conditions, tracks module-specific performance, and helps students pinpoint weak areas.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, professional, and editorial. Evokes structured clarity and calm confidence. Clean typography, deliberate accents (orange/amber), and high readability.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboard templates, excessive glassmorphism, over-rounded ghost cards, cluttered widgets, or cartoonish gamification.

## Design Principles
1. **Focus on Timed Precision**: Interfaces should highlight timing, progression, and state transitions without being anxiety-inducing.
2. **Actionable Module Metrics**: Visual data (like the proficiency radar) must be immediately legible and translate directly into study actions.
3. **Structured Editorial Layout**: Content and stats should feel like a premium textbook or clean digital test client, using generous but structured spacing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High readability contrast (minimum 4.5:1), keyboard-navigable test client, and accommodating reduced motion preferences.
